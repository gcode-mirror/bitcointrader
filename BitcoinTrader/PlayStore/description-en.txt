This app allows you to trade bitcoins at the currently biggest online exchange: MtGox.

All you need is an account at MtGox and this app!

Features:

&bull; Trade in differenct currencies
&bull; Display price charts (via bitcoincharts.com)
&bull; Access your account history
&bull; Login using the MtGox Application Access or API Key
&bull; Get notifications about executed orders
&bull; Widgets with account and ticker information

The source-code of this app is available at https://code.google.com/p/bitcointrader/ .
